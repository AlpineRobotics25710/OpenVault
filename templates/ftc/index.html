About OpenVault

OpenVault is your go-to resource for open-source content tailored for FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) teams. Whether you're a rookie team seeking guidance to get started or an experienced team looking for fresh inspiration, we provide a platform to explore and leverage open-source resources shared by the community.

Share Your Contributions

At OpenVault, we believe in collaboration and the power of shared knowledge. If you'd like to contribute your open-source content, please submit it through our easy-to-use submission form. Help empower the FTC community by sharing your resources and ideas!
